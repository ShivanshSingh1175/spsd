Iphone 16 Pro Max

The iPhone 16 Pro Max offers a 6.9-inch ProMotion OLED display with a smooth 120Hz refresh rate and is powered by the A18 Pro chip for enhanced performance and efficiency. Its advanced camera system includes a 48MP main and Ultra Wide lens, plus a 5x optical zoom Telephoto lens, delivering 4K video at 120 fps with Dolby Vision. It supports USB-C charging, Wi-Fi 7, and improved connectivity with a second-gen Ultra Wideband chip, making it ideal for professional-level photography and productivity.

One Plus 12

The OnePlus 12 is a flagship smartphone featuring a stunning 6.82-inch LTPO AMOLED display with a 2K resolution and 120Hz refresh rate, delivering exceptional visuals with a peak brightness of 4500 nits. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 chipset, it offers top-tier performance for gaming, multitasking, and media consumption. The device is equipped with a versatile triple-camera system, highlighted by a 50MP main sensor, a 48MP ultra-wide lens, and a 64MP 3x periscope telephoto lens. The OnePlus 12 also includes super-fast 100W wired charging, 50W wireless charging, and a large 5400mAh battery, ensuring long-lasting usage.
